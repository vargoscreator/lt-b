Here's How It Works

  1. Select your membership level by choosing how many people you want to send gifts to and amount of rewards you’d like to get.
  2. Register on our website to access your back office and place orders for these gifts.
  3. Receive your first rewards from those orders in your wallet.
  4. We ship these gifts to your people on your behalf and include a letter from you to them with unique invitation number so when they register we know they are a part of your group.
  5. Your people receive your gifts and register on our web site.
  6. We inform you by email of every new registration in your group.
  7. Once again the rewards are being deposited in your wallet from the gifts your people order for their people.
  8. Now people of your people receive their gifts, register, send gifts to their people and you get rewarded again and again, and again 12 levels down, and a total of 29% from the price of those gifts will be rewarded to you and 1% to the charity of your choice.
