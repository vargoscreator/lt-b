Frequently asked questions about EcoZone:

How does the filterless EcoZone purify the air?

The EcoZone air purifier generates activated oxygen (ozone). Ozone is a universal natural oxidizing agent that effectively purifies the air of unpleasant odors, chemicals, viruses, bacteria hundreds of times more efficiently than any other technology. Ozone from EcoHiTek (EcoQuest, Vollara) is drastically different from the ozone generated by any other devices, in that it is pleasant and does not cause any harm or discomfort.

How to prepare EcoZone for operation?

The device is easy to use and is ready to operate immediately after plugging into the socket.

Does EcoZone have any replacement parts that need to be changed over time?

There are no replacement parts.

Is it necessary to clean the device itself?

Filterless technology implies that contaminants do not settle inside the device: instead, purification process takes place in the indoor air. Still, a small film of air impurities can form on the blocks inside the unit due to the air passing through it. It depends on the degree of pollution of the environment in which the device operates. We recommend occasionally cleaning it with a vacuum cleaner for maintenance.
